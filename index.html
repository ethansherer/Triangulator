Triangulator

Welcome to the Triangulator! An easy-to-use tool that allows you to triangulate anything from two different positions.

To use this tool:

1. Enter Google Street View using the icon and locate something of interest. Center your crosshairs on the object and click "Set Pos 1".

2. Move to another position in Street View and center your crosshairs on the same object. Click "Set Pos 2" and the triangulated position will be calculated automatically.

Congratulations! You can now view your triangulated point by exiting Street View.
